General Rules

  • Each college may send only one team to represent them.
  • All participants must carry their College ID and a Deputation Letter from their institution.
  • The performance order will be decided through an online lot system draw.
  • Teams must report to the college by 10:00 AM and be present at the performance area at least 20 minutes before their slot.
  • Teams are responsible for managing their technical aspects. The host college will not be liable for technical issues.
  • Lunch will be provided in the college canteen.
  • Maximum number of participants per team: 20 members.
  • Performances should be based on a theme of your choice – social, cultural, historical, or contemporary.
  • Performance duration: 18 minutes.
  • Stage setup time: 2 minutes.
  • Teams may showcase a fusion of dance, drama, music, or other creative art forms.
  • Both classical and contemporary styles are allowed.
  • Performances should respect Indian culture and traditions.
  • Use of fire, water, live birds/animals, or any hazardous materials on stage is strictly prohibited.
  • Content that hurts religious, cultural, or individual sentiments is not allowed.
  • Depiction of sacred rituals on stage is prohibited.
  • Music tracks must be in MP3 format and brought on a pen drive.
  • Props must be portable and managed by the team. The host college will not provide materials.
  • Teams must clear the stage immediately after their performance.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

🎥 Reel Making Competition Rules

  • Reels must be submitted before 3:00 PM on the event day.
  • Participants must upload the reel on Instagram and collaborate with: @sms.college and @sms.swc.
  • This is strictly an individual event.
  • This event will NOT be considered for overall competition prizes.
  • Judging will be based on creativity, originality, concept and presentation.
  • Views, likes or shares will NOT be considered for evaluation.
  • Content must be decent and respectful.
  • Offensive, political or inappropriate content will lead to disqualification.
  • The judges’ decision will be final and binding.
