🎥 Reel Making Competition

Show your creativity through short and engaging Instagram reels

Submission Deadline

All reels must be submitted to the assigned officials before 3:00 PM on the event day. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Mandatory Collaboration

Participants must upload the reel on Instagram and collaborate with the official handles:

  • @sms.college
  • @sms.swc

Individual Participation

This is strictly an individual event. Team entries are not allowed and the event will not affect overall competition prizes.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity
  • Originality
  • Concept
  • Presentation

The number of views, likes, or shares will NOT be considered for evaluation.

Content Guidelines

  • Content must be decent and respectful.
  • No offensive, political, or inappropriate content.
  • Any violation may lead to disqualification.

Decision Authority

The judges’ decision will be final and binding.
