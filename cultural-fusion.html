Saint Mary's Syrian College, Brahmavar

Cultural Fusion Performance

A celebration of dance, drama, music and creative expression

General Rules

  • Each college may send only one team.
  • Participants must carry College ID and deputation letter.
  • The performance order will be decided through an online lot system.
  • Teams must report to the college by 10:00 AM.
  • Lunch will be provided in the college canteen.

Team Composition

Maximum number of participants per team: 20 members

Theme

Performances should be based on a theme of your choice: social, cultural, historical, or contemporary.

Time Limit

  • Performance Duration: 18 minutes
  • Stage Setup Time: 2 minutes

Performance Format

  • Teams may showcase a fusion of dance, drama, music, or other creative art forms.
  • Both classical and contemporary styles are allowed.
  • Performances must respect Indian culture and traditions.

Strictly Prohibited

  • Use of fire, water, live birds/animals, or hazardous materials.
  • Content that hurts religious, cultural, or individual sentiments.
  • Depiction of sacred rituals on stage.
